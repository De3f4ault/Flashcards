Help Center

Everything you need to know about using FlashCards

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" in the navigation bar and fill out the registration form with your username, email, and password. Your account will be created immediately.

What can I do with FlashCards?

You can create flashcard decks, study with intelligent spaced repetition, track your progress, share decks with others, and browse community-created content.

Creating Decks

How do I create a new deck?

Go to "My Decks" and click "Create New Deck". Give your deck a name and description, then choose whether to make it public or private.

What's the difference between public and private decks?

Private decks are only visible to you. Public decks can be viewed and copied by other users, helping to build a community knowledge base.

How do I add cards to my deck?

You can add cards individually using the "Add Card" button, or use bulk import to add multiple cards at once using the format: "Front text | Back text"

Studying

What are the different study modes?
  • Random: Cards in random order
  • Difficulty: Sorted by difficulty level
  • Accuracy: Sorted by your performance
  • Least Studied: Cards you've practiced least
  • Chronological: By creation date
How does the difficulty system work?

Cards start with difficulty 1 and adjust based on your performance. Cards you get wrong become easier (lower difficulty), while cards you consistently get right become harder.

What should I do with cards I need to review?

Use the "Review" feature to focus on cards with low accuracy or high difficulty. This helps you spend time on material that needs the most attention.

Managing Cards

How do I edit or delete cards?

Go to your deck and click "Manage Cards". From there you can edit or delete individual cards.

Can I import cards from a file?

Yes! Use the bulk import feature. Format your cards as "Front | Back" with one card per line.

Account Settings

How do I change my password?

Go to your profile settings and click "Change Password". You'll need to enter your current password and your new password.

Can I change my username or email?

Yes, you can update both your username and email address in your profile settings.

Troubleshooting

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Currently, please contact support to reset your password. A self-service password reset feature is coming soon.

My study session isn't working properly

Try refreshing the page. If the problem persists, make sure your browser has JavaScript enabled and try clearing your browser cache.

I found a bug or have a feature request

Please contact our support team with details about the issue or your feature suggestion. We appreciate your feedback and are constantly working to improve FlashCards.

Still Need Help?

Our support team is here to assist you with any questions or issues.
